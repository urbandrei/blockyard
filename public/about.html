Blockyard

A puzzle game where you place factories on a grid to manufacture shapes, then design and share your own levels to stump your friends.

Play now

How it works

Each Blockyard level is a small factory floor. You drop factory tiles onto a grid, point them at one another with funnels, and watch shapes flow through your contraption. The goal is to manufacture the shapes the level asks for, in the right colors and the right order.

There are nine shape types in total: three forms (circle, square, triangle) in three colors. Combine them, sort them, and route them across the playable area to clear the buffer.

Build your own

Every Blockyard level can be cracked open in the editor. The same tiles, funnels, and flow rules that ship with the game are the ones you use to author your own puzzles. When your design works (and stumps you a little), share the level with one tap. Friends play it from a link, no account required.

Daily featured

A new community-made level is spotlighted every day. Beat it, keep your streak alive, and try the back catalog when you want a deeper dive.
